Marcus Aurelius
Meditations


  1. “Understand that your time has a limit set to it. Use it, then to advance your enlightenment; or it will be gone, and never in your power again.” - Marcus Aurelius, Meditations

  2. “The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way.” - Marcus Aurelius, Meditations

  3. “And a commitment to justice in your own acts. Which means: thought and action resulting in the common good. What you were born to do.” - Marcus Aurelius, Meditations

  4. “You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realise this, and you will find strength.” - Marcus Aurelius, Meditations
